SynSpeech Dataset Versions

SynSpeech is a synthetic speech dataset created for standardized evaluation of disentangled speech representations, with controlled variations in speaker identity, spoken content, and emotional tone. It includes over 160,000 utterances generated with neural text-to-speech models, annotated with speaker identity, text, prosody, and tone (neutral, happy, angry). By controlling individual factors, SynSpeech supports isolated evaluations of model performance on disentanglement tasks.
